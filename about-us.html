About

What started as an idea turned into a reality, Shoes Me is all about providing up to date news about sneakers to its consumers. Not only providing latest news about sneakers but a step-to-step guide on what to look for in purchasing their favorite sneakers. Shoes Me may be a relatively new site, but is committed to being one of the top source for sneaker news and to become a household name one day.

From having a few thousand users on launch to having our highest concurrent users recently at 1 million, setting a personal record and we are not just stopping there and a total of 8 million followers across social media, Shoes Me is definitely getting its recognition and will only to continue to grow from there. Shoes Me was founded in 2020 by Dave Pua and currently resides in Malabon, Philippines.
